$$| A \cup B | = | A | + | B | - | A \cap B |,$$
$$| A \cup B \cup C | = | A | + | B | + | C | 
  - | A \cap B | - | B \cap C | - | A \cap C |
  + | A \cap B \cap C |$$ In general,
$$| A_1 \cup A_2 \cup ... \cup A_{n} |$$ $$= \sum_{i} | A_{i} |$$
$$- \sum_{i<j} | A_{i} \cap A_{j} |$$
$$+ \sum_{i<j<k} | A_{i} \cap A_{j} \cap A_{k} |$$
$$- ... + (-1)^{n - 1 } | A_1 \cap A_2 \cap ... \cap A_{n} |$$
