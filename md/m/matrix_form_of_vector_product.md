The vector product of two vectors can be written in the form of a
matrix; if vector $ a = \left ( 
\begin{array}{c} 
  a_1 \\
  a_2 \\
  a_3 
\end{array} 
\right ) , $ etc., then: $$a \times b = \left | 
\begin{array}{ccc}  
  i & a_1 & b_1 \\
  j & a_2 & b_2 \\
  k & a_3 & b_3 
\end{array} 
\right | .$$
