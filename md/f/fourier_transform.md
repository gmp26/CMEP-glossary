For a function f, the Fourier transform of f is:
$$\mathrm{F(t)} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} \int _{- \infty} ^{\infty}
\mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{itx}} \mathrm{f(x)dx} .$$

Note that this implies:
$$\mathrm{f(x)} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi}} \int _{- \infty} ^{\infty}
\mathrm{e}^{ - \mathrm{itx}} \mathrm{F(t)dt} .$$
