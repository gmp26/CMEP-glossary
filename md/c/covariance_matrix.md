For data consisting of n-tuples of values $(x_{1}...x_{n}),$ we can
compute the covariance $C_{ij}$ of any pair of variables $x_{i}, x_{j}.$
The matrix $ 
C = 
\left( 
\begin{array}{ccc}
  C_{11} & \ldots & C_{1n} \\
  \vdots & & \vdots \\ 
  C_{n1} & \ldots & C_{nn} 
\end{array} 
\right) 
$ whose entries are these covariances, is called the covariance matrix
for the data.
